Modern Farming

We are a small cafe shop in the heart of the Scottish Borders, we focus on providing our customers with high quality Organic produce while taking care of the environment, which is why we offer you the eco, sustainable and healthy products. To encourage our clients to live a healthy life, we also organize gardening courses for beginners. By Modern agricultural practices, we are able to increase our productivity while reducing our environmental impact.
